Comparison of .png and .jpg

Ways that .pngs are superior to .jpgs

  • Because it supports transparency, it's perfect for web design and overlays.
  • Lossless compression guarantees unaltered high-quality photos.
  • Better suited for pictures like schematics and logos that have text or sharp edges.

Ways that .pngs are inferior to .jpgs

  • Larger files than JPGs, which may cause load times to increase.
  • For photos with intricate gradients, it is less effective.
